**A:**

### Q: "Isn't 5G old technology?"

**A:**

**Key Points:**
- 5G is mid-lifecycle (peak deployment)
- 4G analogy: lasted 11 years, 5G will too
- Industry focused on improving 5G now

### Q: "How is this 5G-specific?"

**A:**

>
